About

Mr. Chandee Molina, a 41-year-old religious preacher at One Way- Cordova Cebu, was raised in a profound religious household and was born in Tagum City, Davao. Eleonor Maxillom Molina, a devout lady from Soong, Lapu-lapu City, was his wife. They were fortunate to have two competent children, a boy and a girl, who are both responsible, dependable, and gifted thanks to God's will and grace, which have continued to carry them since then.One of his favorite verses is can be found in Matthew 28:19, which states "Go therefore & make disciples of all the Nations.".He was the father of faith in One Way-Cordova, a preacher, outreacher and the founder of One Way-Gilutungan and Caubian. An all rounder instrumentalist way back before he became a pastor and an active advocate of youth. Under his supervision and guidance towards every member he was able to produce over 2 licensed pastors for this year. Additionally, his youth department was able to brought home the bacon during the Area Youth Convention held in San Remegio, Cebu and even his LIHI department was able to bagged the crown during their LIHI Fellowship.

I have admired this man of faith since 2018 due to the fact that he is very transparent, vigilant and treats his member equally despite the status of an individual. His life is like a living testimony and will always be a living testimony just like his father's. The continuous development and improvement of his supervised church, is also a living proof of how devoted and what a good and faithful servant can do. I also admired this pastor because of his unselfishness, patience and love towards his members and people. It is indeed a privilige to be supervised by a faithful and fierce servant of God. As what the Mateo 6:33 has said which what he also reminds us, "Seek ye first the Kingdom Of God and his Righteousness, and all this things shall be added unto you".
