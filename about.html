ABOUT US

Good Day! We are the Team Alpha Bros. We are a team comprised of two individuals, Frank Alcordo and Honey Margarette Almojallas, both BSIT first year students in Cordova Public College. The team's name ALPHA BROS was inspired from the first two common letters of the member's surnames which is the 'AL'. The following letters of the team's name which is the 'PH' was inspired also from the first letters of the member's first names, the letters 'F' and 'H'. The term "alpha" refers to something that is first or something or someone designated with the name alpha or the Greek letter α especially denoting the first in position, order, or class. Both of our member's names were to be found on the first rows or lists in their class masterlists, which explains why our team was named after the term Alpha. Furthermore, the term "bros" in the ALPHA BROS, specifies the fellowship and symbiosis that the members share in this team.

Team ALPHA BROS primarily creates websites, webpages, and other online content for those who needs and desires it. Our team creates websites and pages from beginner-level to moderate-level designs. Also, the team has adequate proficiency with HTML and CSS to create the websites and webpages you desire at a very reasonable costs.
